Market Research & Strategy

A roadmap for INCH to become the world’s leading biometric coaching platform for elite clients and their trainers.

Market Insights

The wearable and personal training market is booming. Most health data captured by devices like Oura, Apple Watch and Fitbit remains unused due to poor integration and lack of actionable insights. Clients—especially high‑performers—demand AI‑powered coaching and holistic wellness guidance that extends beyond workouts to nutrition, recovery and mental health.

  • Growing market: the personal training industry is projected to reach $85 billion by 2035 with digital platforms driving growth.
  • High‑end potential: executives and ultra‑rich clients seek premium, time‑efficient coaching experiences.
  • Data fragmentation: disparate apps and wearables make it hard to see a unified picture of health.

Identified Gaps

Our research reveals unmet needs that current platforms fail to address:

  • Unified dashboard: trainers need a single, elegant interface that aggregates data across devices and apps.
  • Actionable insights: raw data is meaningless without AI that interprets patterns and predicts behaviours.
  • Elite client focus: few tools cater to the unique expectations of CEOs and high‑net‑worth individuals.
  • Holistic wellness: stress and mental‑health metrics must be integrated with physical performance.
  • Smart scheduling: linking health data with calendars to anticipate stressful events and adjust plans is virtually nonexistent.

Strategic Pillars

To lead the market, INCH will innovate across several dimensions:

  • Data integration: build secure APIs to connect all major wearables and health apps, presenting a 360‑degree view.
  • AI & predictive coaching: deploy machine learning models that turn data into personalised recommendations and proactive alerts.
  • Luxury concierge: offer white‑glove support and personalised nutrition, recovery and stress‑management plans for elite clientele.
  • Holistic wellness: integrate mental‑health analytics and mindfulness practices alongside training metrics.
  • Partnerships & community: collaborate with premium device makers and create educational content to build brand authority.

Subscription Model

Our tiered pricing caters to trainers at different stages of growth while emphasising value for high‑end clients:

  • Elite Starter – $49/month: up to 5 clients, unified dashboard, basic AI insights and email support.
  • Professional – $99/month: up to 20 clients, advanced AI, nutrition & calendar integration, and priority support.
  • Premier – $199/month: up to 50 clients, mental‑health analytics, API access, custom branding and priority support.
  • Executive Concierge – $399/month: unlimited clients, concierge wellness service, dedicated AI health advisor and VIP support.
